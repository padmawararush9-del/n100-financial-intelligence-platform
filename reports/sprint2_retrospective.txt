Sprint 2 focused on building the financial analytics engine for the N100 Financial Intelligence Platform.

Implemented profitability ratios (NPM, OPM, ROE, ROCE, ROA), leverage and efficiency ratios (D/E, ICR, Asset Turnover), CAGR growth metrics, cash flow KPIs, and capital allocation analysis.

Key decisions included handling zero denominators with None values, suppressing leverage warnings for Financials companies, and using computed ratio-engine values as the source of truth while retaining source values for display purposes.

Major edge cases addressed included turnaround scenarios, decline-to-loss situations, debt-free companies, zero-base CAGR calculations, and source-data anomalies identified through validation checks.
